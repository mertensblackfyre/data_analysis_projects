# Project 1 - FIFA 21

Cleaned, analayzed & visualized raw dataset of EA Sports installment of their FIFA series.

I answered the following question:

-  Who are top 10 rated players?
-  Who are the highest paid players?
-  Who are the most valued players?
-  Which country has the most players?
-  Does age affect player rating?
-  Does high valued players get paid more?


Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yagunnersya/fifa-21-messy-raw-dataset-for-cleaning-exploring/data
